Here's a time line of Mrs. Lewis's life

    1903 - Born in rural Yarmouth Maud Dowley.
    1913 - Drop out of school to get away from her school mates' constant mockery due to her juvenile rheumatoid arthritis disability.
    1937 - Persuded Mr. Everett Lewis, an itinerant fish peddler, to hire her as a lived-in maid despite her disability.
    1938 - Married to Mr. Everett Lewis
    1939 - Sells her first painting while working as a night watchman at the "Poor Farm."
    1965 - Maud is featured on CBC-TV broadcast and in numerous newspaper stories which brought her world wide publicity.
    1968 - Maud's health deteriorates after her hip fractue from a fall.
    1970 - Maud passes away on July 30th.
